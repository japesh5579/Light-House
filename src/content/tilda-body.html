Answers to popular questions

We are devoted to the traditional path of Hatha Yoga, embracing its essence and ancient wisdom. Occasionally, in harmony with the flow of the class, we gently introduce elements from the primary series of Ashtanga Yoga, allowing you to explore different dimensions of your practice.

We place great emphasis on internal awareness and alignment. As you delve into the world of asanas, you will not only master the postures but also learn to create your own sequences and share the gift of yoga with others. We also embrace the power of subtle energy practices, pranayama, and meditation. These practices serve as gateways to deeper self-discovery and inner transformation. Through these ancient techniques, you will uncover the profound connection between mind, body, and spirit, fostering a harmonious balance within.

The courses we run are as interactive and practical as possible. We provide plenty of opportunities for teaching practice, including two practical exams with feedback from peers and tutors to help you develop as a teacher.

To apply for an Indian eVisa, travelers need to complete an online application form, providing personal information, travel details, and uploading supporting documents, such as a passport-sized photograph and a scanned copy of the passport's bio page. The application is then processed by the Indian authorities, who assess the information and make a decision regarding the visa approval.

Upon approval, the eVisa is electronically linked to the traveler's passport and sent via email. It is essential to carry a printed copy of the eVisa during the journey, as it will be verified by immigration officers upon arrival in India.

E-TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENTS: The India e-Visa Online Document Requirements to apply for an e-Tourist Visa online are:

  • Passport valid for at least 6 months from the intended arrival date in India, and two blank pages.
  • An image of the passport biographical page.
  • A recent applicant's photograph, passport-style
  • Credit or debit card to pay the India online visa fee
  • Current email address to receive the approved India Tourist e-Visa document.

Payment Terms:

Pricing

The prices displayed on our website and advertisements reflect our Early Bird pricing. Early Bird rates are available until 60 days before the training start date. After this period, standard pricing will apply.

Early Bird Pricing Policy

The Early Bird rate is available only to students who:
  • Book their training more than 60 days before the course start date.
  • Complete full payment before the Early Bird deadline (60 days prior to the training start date).
The Early Bird rate does not apply to:
  • Bookings made within 60 days of the course start date.
  • Students who have not completed full payment by the Early Bird deadline.
  • Payment plans or installment options extending beyond the Early Bird deadline.
Once a course reaches 60 days before commencement, all unpaid balances will automatically revert to the standard course price.

Payment Options

Option A — Full Payment (Early Bird Eligible)
Students who pay in full before the Early Bird deadline will receive:
  • The Early Bird rate.
  • Immediate booking confirmation.

Option B — Deposit Payment
Students may choose to pay a deposit only ($500 for 200HR courses or $555 for 300HR courses) to temporarily reserve their place.
Important: A deposit payment does not fully confirm a student's place. The student's spot is temporarily reserved until full payment is received. Final confirmation is granted only upon full payment.
Students who pay a deposit may still receive the Early Bird rate, provided the remaining balance is paid in full before the Early Bird deadline (60 days before the training start date).

Option C — Installment Payment Plan
Students who choose to pay a deposit may divide the remaining balance into up to three payments.
All balances must be fully paid at least 2 months before the training start date. This allows us to finalize logistics, accommodation, venue arrangements, and student confirmations.
Please note that installment plans extending beyond the Early Bird deadline will be charged at the standard course price.

Payment Deadlines

  • Full payment must be received no later than 60 days before the course start date to qualify for Early Bird pricing.
  • All outstanding balances must be settled at least 60 days before the training start date.
  • Bookings made within 60 days of the course start date require full payment at the standard course price at the time of booking.

Promotional Pricing

Lighthouse Krishna reserves the right to offer discounts, promotions, and campaigns as deemed appropriate. Prices may vary during promotional periods, and individual students' payments shall not be subject to questioning or comparison.
